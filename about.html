My Passions & Personalitly

Hi, I’m Vincent my directive is to help usher more good ideas into this universe. I do that with creative thinking, illustrating, and programming. Okay and maybe a little magic too.


My longing desire to design stems from my affinity for order and geometry. Due to my attention for detail, I am constantly absorbing ideas from everyday life. I am passionate about my work and driven to satisfy clients.

In a nutshell, I am passionate to create visual stories. I have been doing this since 2017.

Within the last five years, I’ve worked as a salesperson for two companies, MediaMarkt and a motor clothing store. There I’ve got the chance to meet and help several people with their businesses along the way.

Whilst I was working for those companies, I was given the opportunity to create my own company. With lots of thinking, I started my business in May 2021 named: Visual Capture. It only just recently started to pop off, where I went from a single photographer job every other month, to now having 5 large clients, 2 of which are long term. Because of that, there were some goodbye’s and I then quit my job to gain more time, so I could fully commit to this opportunity.

For the upcoming next years, I plan to continue working on my business, as I still consider it to be a rather small business. Also, I mean to finish my Batchelor degree in ICT, and develop myself further into Fintech, DeFi, Blockchain, Tokenomics and Web 3.0.

What do people say about me?

Vincent feels called to serve a greater purpose in life. Thoughtful and idealistic, he strives to have a positive impact on other people, and the world around him. He rarely shy’s away from an opportunity to do the right thing, even when doing so is far easier.

Vincent’s passion and charisma allow him to inspire others not just in their careers but in every arena of their lives, including their relationships.

Nothing brings him a deeper sense of joy and satisfaction than guiding friends and loved ones to be their best selves.

Vincent tends to be vocal about his values, including authenticity and altruism. When something strikes him as unjust or wrong, he speaks up. But rarely comes across as brash or pushy, as his sensitivity and insight guide him to speak in ways that resonate with others.

Vincent’s secret weapon is his purity of intent. He is motivated by a sincere wish to do the right thing rather than a desire to manipulate or have power over other people. Even when he disagrees with someone, he searches for common ground. The result is he can communicate with an eloquence and sensitivity that are nearly impossible to ignore.

And well, that’s me. If you need help with something, and think I would be a good fit, then let’s talk about it. Because maybe I can be of service.
